How It Works

Explore the Simplicity and Efficiency of RealityDiner

For Customers:

  1. Download The App: Start by downloading RealityDiner from your app store.
  2. Browse The Menu: Use your device to view the menu in AR, or wear AR/VR glasses for an immersive experience.

For Restaurants:

  1. Join the Waitlist: Inerested in bringing AR magic to your restaurant? Join our waitlist today! While our current capacity is full, registering ensures you're among the first to bring this innovative experience to your city.
  2. Digital Transformation Team Visit: Once you're up next, our team will visit your restaurant to capture and digitize your dishes. We recommend plating them just as you would serve, ensuring the AR experience is as real as it gets.
  3. QR Code and AR Links: After the shoot, we'll provide you with a unique QR code and online lnks. These can be displayed on your tables or shared with your guests, enabling them to access your AR-enabled menu.
  4. Dashboard for Order Management: Use our intuitive dashboard to manage orders, update menu details, and ensure a seamless dining experience for your guests. It's efficiencyat its best!
